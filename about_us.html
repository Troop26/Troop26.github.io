About Troop 26

Found in 1946, Troop 26 is committed to bettering its scouts and community, through community service,
leadership opportunities for scouts, and skill building to empower scouts to serve (eg, CPR training). If you or your child between 6th grade
and 18 years old is interested in joining us, Contact Us! and/or feel free to attend any of our Wed. night
weekly meetings @ Lyme Fire House taking place during the school year.
